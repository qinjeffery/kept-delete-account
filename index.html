Kept Account Deletion

This page explains how users can request deletion of their Kept account and associated data.

How to request account deletion

To delete your Kept account, email us from the email address associated with your Kept account.

Please include the subject line: Kept Account Deletion Request

Request account deletion

What data will be deleted

When your account is deleted, we delete data associated with your account, including:

  • Account information
  • Promises and goals
  • Daily progress and streak information
  • Reminder preferences
  • App settings

Data we may retain

Some information may be retained if required by law, for security, fraud prevention, dispute resolution, or legitimate business purposes. Any retained information will be kept only as long as necessary.

Processing time

We aim to process account deletion requests within 30 days of verifying the request.
